COMMON QUESTIONS ABOUT RENDER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Will my app always be online?
A: On free tier, it sleeps after 15 min of inactivity (wakes on request).
   Upgrade to paid tier for 24/7 always-on service.

Q: Do I need to configure anything special?
A: Nope! Procfile and render.yaml are already configured for you.
   Just push to GitHub and click deploy.

Q: What if I want a custom domain?
A: Easy! In Render dashboard: Custom Domain → Add your domain name.
   SSL certificate auto-created.

Q: Can I update my app after deploying?
A: Yes! Push to GitHub, Render auto-redeploys. No manual steps!

Q: What if something breaks?
A: Check Render logs for errors. Fix locally, push to GitHub, redeploy.

Q: Is my app data safe?
A: Your app is stateless (no data stored). Safe by design.
   No database = no data breaches.
